How to Use

Pick your rotor starting positions by replacing the A’s with another letter. This will change the encryption. Then type in the text that you want encrypted. Only letters will be encrypted. All punctuation and spaces will be unencrypted to the default letter ‘K’ once it is decrypted. To send a message to someone, choose your rotor start positions and then enter the text and press ‘encrypt.’ To decrypt the message, you must have the starting positions of the rotors. Place them in the respective boxes and then the encrypted message into the input field. Then, press the ‘encrypt’ button and the message will be decrypted. It is important to get the starting positions exactly right otherwise the decryption will not work.
